1. Residential and Commercial Property Management

At Gerton, we understand that managing properties can be time-consuming and challenging. Our Property Management services are designed to take the stress out of renting and leasing, allowing you to enjoy the benefits of property ownership without the headaches. Whether you own family units, apartment complexes, malls, office blocks, Petrol stations, industrial parks and warehouses, our experienced team is here to help you.


Our Residential Property Management Services Include:

  • Tenant Screening & Placement: We use a thorough screening process to ensure we place reliable, responsible tenants in your property. Our screening includes background checks, credit assessments, and rental history reviews.

  • Marketing & Advertising: We market your property across multiple channels to attract a wide pool of qualified renters. From listing on top rental websites to professional photography, we ensure your property stands out.

  • Rent Collection & Financial Management: Our team manages all aspects of rent collection, ensuring timely payments. We also provide detailed financial reporting to help you stay on top of your property's income and expenses.

  • Service Charge Management: We handle all maintenance and repair requests promptly. Our team of trusted contractors ensures that your property stays in top condition, preserving its value and your tenants' satisfaction. We also handle matters waste management, water supply management and security personel engagement, among other operational matters.

Let us take care of the day-to-day operations, so you can focus on what matters most. Whether you're a first-time landlord or a seasoned investor, our residential property management services are tailored to meet your specific needs.

4. Funding of Joint Venture Projects

A Joint Venture (JV) in real estate refers to a partnership between two or more parties who come together to develop or invest in a property. Typically, one party contributes capital, while the other provides expertise, property management, or other essential resources. The parties share both the risks and profits associated with the development project.

We understand the intricacies of real estate joint ventures and can help you structure the right partnership and secure the necessary funding. Our services are tailored to meet the needs of developers, investors, and landowners seeking to unlock the potential of a Joint Venture partnership.


If you're ready to take your real estate development project to the next level, a joint venture can be the perfect solution. Gerton is here to provide the funding, expertise, and support necessary to make your real estate ambitions a reality with a funding ration of upto 70:30.
