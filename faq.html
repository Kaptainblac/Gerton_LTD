Frequently Asked Questions

  1. Home
  2. FAQs
  1. What services do you provide?

    2. We offer a wide range of real estate services, including buying, selling, and renting properties, as well as property management and real
    estate investment advice.

  2. Do you only deal with residential properties?

    3. No, we also handle commercial properties, land sales, and investment opportunities in addition to residential homes, condos, and apartments.

  3. How do I start the process of buying a home?

    4. First, you can browse our listings to get an idea of available properties. Once you find a home you're interested in contact us and we will help
    guide you through the buying process.

  4. Do I need to get pre-approved for a mortgage before buying?

    5. While it’s not required, getting pre-approved for a mortgage can help you understand your budget and show sellers you are a serious buyer.

  5. How long does it take to buy a home?

    6. The home-buying process typically takes around 30 to 45 days from offer acceptance to closing, though it can vary depending on the transaction
    and financing process.

  6. How do I list my property for sale?

    7. Simply contact us and will help you with the listing process, including pricing, staging, and marketing your property to potential buyers.

  7. Can I get an estimate for the value of my property?

    8. Yes, we conduct a comprehensive market analysis, taking into account recent sales of similar properties, the condition of your home and provide
    you with a detailed comparative market analysis (CMA) of your property.

  8. Do you assist with financing options?

    9. While we don’t offer financing directly, we can connect you with trusted mortgage financiers and lenders who can help secure financing for your
    home purchase.

  9. Are there any legal requirements when buying or selling a property?

    10. Yes, there are various legal requirements involved in property transactions, such as disclosures, contracts, and title searches. Our agents will
    help you navigate the legal process to ensure everything is in order.

  10. Can you help me find investment properties?

    11. Yes, we specialize in identifying high-potential investment properties. We will assist you in finding properties that match your investment goals.

  11. What should I look for when investing in real estate?

    12. Some of the factors to consider inlude; rental income potential, location, and opportunities for appreciation. Our team can provide expert advice
    based on market trends and property evaluations.
