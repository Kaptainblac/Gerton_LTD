Property Description

Artesano Apartments are an exceptional property that introduce excellent designs and elegance to the Kenyan residential property market. When you mention flair, novelty and the perfection of simplicity, Artesano Apartments immediately spring to mind.

Located 200 metres off Mombasa Road in Kenya's Syokimau neighborhood, the Artesano Apartments are a prestigious flagship building that introduces sophisticated styles and elegance to the real estate market with options of Two, Three and Four-bedroom units.
